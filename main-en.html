LA BARONíA

—————— // ——————

La Baronía is located in the picturesque town of Sant Feliu
de Codines, which was, during the 20th century, one of the summer
resorts of the Catalan bourgeoisie. Today, many of these houses
are still owned by the same families.

Sant Feliu de Codines is located in the Catalan region of Valles
Oriental. This region is an elongated valley parallel to the coast,
separated from the coast by a series of mountains and sierras,
such as Montseny or Montnegre.
